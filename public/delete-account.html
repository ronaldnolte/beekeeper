Delete Your Account

BeekTools Beekeeper · Last updated: June 17, 2026

This page explains how to delete your BeekTools Beekeeper account and the data associated with it. You may request deletion at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email from the address associated with your account to:

Email a deletion request

Address: ron.nolte@gmail.com
Subject: Delete My BeekTools Account

Please send the request from the email address you use to sign in, so we can verify ownership of the account. We will process your request and confirm by email, typically within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your account and sign-in credentials.
  • Your apiaries, including their names, locations, and notes.
  • Your hives and all related records — inspections, interventions, tasks, varroa tests, and snapshots.
  • Any weather forecast records tied to your apiaries.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Aggregated, non-identifying satellite and weather data retrieved from third-party services is not tied to your identity and is not affected.

Questions

For any questions about account or data deletion, contact us at ron.nolte@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
